### Privacy Policy

We **do not collect**, access, store, or share any personally identifiable information (such as name, address, phone number, email, etc.). This app operates entirely offline/decentralized, and all experiences are processed locally on your device.

#### 2. Use of Device Permissions
* **Motion Sensors (Gyroscope & Accelerometer)**: This app accesses your device sensors solely to detect the tilt angle of the device for real-time calculation and adjustment of the smoke's physical flow direction.

This app does not include any third-party advertising tracking, behavior tracking, or data analysis tools. Your usage habits and behaviors remain completely private.
